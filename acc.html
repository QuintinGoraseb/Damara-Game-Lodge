Good day everyone and welcome to the Covid-19 Relief Fund site, where i'am raising money to fight the ills of the Covid-19 pandemic. My goal with this fundme site is to raise enough money to help out single mothers battling the pandemic as well as vulnarable children like orphans. The money raised will be directed to initiatives to help better the lives of these two targeted groups of people.

There is a shortage of mask and testing equipment for underprivileged children and that gap needs to be filled. This will be possible with your donations and contributions. Let us come together and help better the lives of these unfortunate people and make a difference in the world. Any donation amount large or small will be greatly appreciated and will do a world of good for these people. Thank you and god bless you.
